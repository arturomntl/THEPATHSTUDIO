PATH STUDIO

A digital design studio that helps build, design, and guide those who want to venture onto the digital path.

We are not those who treat design or creative-thinking as commodities, or those who offer cookie-cutter solutions.

We are creatives with a purpose in life: to use our creative problem-solving to guide those on the creative path. The path that nowadays calls for what we love to create: unique branding, graphics, web-designs, and websites. We are PATH studio: the studio for your path, walk with us.

THE FOUNDERS

The path that Arturo Montiel and Justin Kirchon traversed to create their studio into reality starts all the way back 2015. When they met as kids who loved photography/photshop. These two grew up having photoshoots and sessions of digital creation and publication.

Eventually their passions would be fueled by purpose which guided them on their paths to meet again in college to create PATH STUDIO LLC.
